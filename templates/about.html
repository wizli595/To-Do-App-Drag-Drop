Welcome to the Task Board App!

Task Board is your ultimate productivity companion, designed to streamline task management and collaboration. Whether you're working solo or in a team, this app helps you keep track of your tasks with ease.

Core Features

  • **Task Management:** Add, edit, and delete tasks effortlessly.
  • **Drag & Drop:** Move tasks seamlessly across columns.
  • **User Authentication:** Secure login and signup with local storage.
  • **Dark Mode:** Switch between light and dark themes.
  • **Persistent Storage:** Tasks and user data are stored locally.

How It Works

  1. Sign Up / Log In: Create an account or log in with your credentials.
  2. Create Tasks: Add tasks to the 'To Do' column.
  3. Organize Tasks: Drag and drop tasks into 'In Progress' or 'Done' columns.
  4. Manage Tasks: Edit or delete tasks anytime.
  5. Logout: Securely log out of your account.
